Practical static limits

Static hosting breaks when you need protected data, coordinated multi-user state, or server-grade compute. It does not break when you want dramatic visuals, responsive dashboards, narrative transitions, or rich local interaction.

In other words, the ceiling is higher than “simple brochure site.” The real constraints are persistence, secrecy, and compute ownership, not polish.
